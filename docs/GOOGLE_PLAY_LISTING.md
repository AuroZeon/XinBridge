#### 英文 (en-US)

```
XinBridge is a gentle companion app for people living with cancer. Track wellness on your device, talk with an AI companion powered by OpenAI (ChatGPT API) or a configured cloud provider when you agree and are online—no account required for basic use.

WHAT XINBRIDGE OFFERS

• Mood Check-in — Record how you feel and your pain level. Your data stays on your device, ready to share with your doctor at your next visit.

• Heart Chat — Supportive replies via cloud AI (OpenAI ChatGPT API or configured provider). Internet and one-time in-app consent required; built-in messages if you decline or when offline. Conversations are saved on your device: start a new chat, browse past chats, and continue where you left off.

• Symptom Log — Track fatigue, nausea, pain, sleep, and appetite. Smart insights help you prepare questions for your doctor.

• Questions for Doctor — Write down what you want to ask before appointments. Walk in confident, leave nothing unasked.

• Breathing — 4-7-8 guided breathing. Cool-down mode when you need to steady yourself fast.

• Quick SOS — Hard moments? Get 5-4-3-2-1 grounding instantly. Reach out to family with one tap.

• Night Sanctuary — Real rain, ocean, and forest sounds. Breathing ball, body scan, sleep stories. OLED black, gentle on the eyes when you can't sleep.

• Hope Library — Stories of hope by cancer type (from web/third-party; not verified for accuracy).

• Zen Toys — Flow sand, connect stars, breathe in sync. Small games, big calm.

• Family Contact — Notify loved ones. Write what's hard to say out loud.

PRIVACY & SAFETY

• No account required. No mandatory login.
• AI chat sends conversation text to cloud providers (e.g. OpenAI) only after you agree. Symptom and mood logs stay on your device unless you use other networked features.
• Symptom and mood data stored on your device only.
• Designed with input from patients and caregivers.

No cancer patient should feel alone at night. XinBridge is here for you.
```

• 症状记录 — 记录疲惫、恶心、疼痛、睡眠与食欲。智能分析助您为复诊做好准备。

隐私与安全

• 无需注册账号。
• 心语对话在同意后发送至云端 AI 处理；症状等记录默认仅存本机。
• 症状与情绪数据仅存于本机。
• 在设计过程中参考了患者与照护者反馈。

不让任何癌症患者在夜晚感到孤单。心桥在这里陪伴你。
