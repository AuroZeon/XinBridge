## English (en)

**Tagline**

*The world is quiet now, and you are safe in this moment.*

**Short intro**

XinBridge (心桥) is a specialized “Calm Tech” sanctuary designed specifically for cancer patients navigating the emotional complexities of treatment, recovery, and those quiet, difficult hours of the night.

Developed with a focus on sleep support, context-aware AI, and empathetic UX, XinBridge is more than a tracker—it is a bridge to peace.

**Core features**

**The Night Sanctuary**  
A dedicated OLED-first sleep environment. Red-shift eye protection, a synchronized 4-2-6 Breathing Orb, and a layered Soundscape Engine (brown noise, rainfall, forest winds, ocean tides), plus body scan and sleep stories when rest won’t come.

**Intelligent AI companion (Heart Chat)**  
When you choose to use cloud AI and you’re online, XinBridge offers a **Gentle Listener** tone that adapts to the moment—powered by **OpenAI (ChatGPT API)** or another **configured cloud provider** you approve in app. Your **chat threads are saved on this device**: start a **new conversation**, open **past chats**, and pick up where you left off—so you don’t have to repeat yourself. From the night flow, **Night Companion** leans into quiet, grounding language (including softer, shorter lines) for those 3 AM hours.

**Zen Toys & interactive play**  
Five physics-based mini-games designed for flow-state calm—from fluid sand (“Stardust Flow”) to constellation drawing (“Celestial Architect”), plus breathe-in-sync, geometric garden, and light painting—with tactile, haptic-friendly interactions.

**Symptom & mood harmony**  
An approachable symptom log with an interactive **Body Map**. Track fatigue, nausea, pain, sleep, and appetite without the cold-clinic feel. XinBridge notices patterns and can nudge you to prepare questions for your next visit.

**The Library of Hope**  
A global-facing collection of recovery stories from cancer communities (e.g. US, Canada, China). Refresh to discover new stories—offered for encouragement, not medical advice.

**Quick SOS**  
Immediate access to **5-4-3-2-1 grounding** and a calm path to **family contact**, plus space to capture thoughts you aren’t ready to say out loud.

**What makes XinBridge different?**

**Privacy-aware by design**  
Mood and symptom logs stay on your device for everyday use. **Heart Chat** sends message text to a cloud AI **only after you explicitly agree** in the app; you can still use **built-in supportive messages** if you decline or when you’re offline. Saved **conversation history stays on your device** so you can reopen threads—nothing magical about “all AI on-phone,” but clarity about what leaves the device and what doesn’t.

**Context-aware**  
The app responds to time of day and flow—e.g. late-night emphasis on sleep tools and gentler chat when you arrive from Night Sanctuary.

**Tactile healing**  
Interactions are tuned with Capacitor haptics and Framer Motion so the UI feels steady and reassuring, not frantic.

## 中文 (zh-Hans)

**一句话**

*此刻世界安静，你安全地待在这一刻。*

**简介**

心桥（XinBridge）是为癌症患者打造的「温和科技」陪伴空间，陪伴你走过治疗与康复中的情绪波动，以及深夜里那些难眠、难说的时刻。

我们重视睡眠支持、情境感知与有温度的交互——心桥不只是记录工具，更是一座通往平静的心桥。

**核心功能**

**夜晚陪伴（Night Sanctuary）**  
面向 OLED 的深色与红光护眼、同步 **4-2-6 呼吸球**、多层 **声景**（褐噪声、雨声、林风、海浪），以及身体扫描与睡眠故事，在睡不着时有一处专门留给你的角落。

**AI 心语（Heart Chat）**  
在您**同意**且联网时，通过 **OpenAI（ChatGPT API）** 或部署方配置的**兼容云端接口**生成回复，人格温和、倾听为主。对话支持 **本机保存**：可 **新建对话**、查看 **以往对话**、**接着聊**，减少重复倾诉的负担。从夜晚陪伴进入时，可启用偏深夜、偏 grounding 的 **Night Companion** 风格。

**禅趣玩具**  
五款基于物理与触控的小游戏，帮助进入心流、打发等候时间、缓解焦虑——含流沙、星座连线、呼吸同步、几何花园与光绘等。

**症状与情绪**  
直观的 **身体地图** 与症状记录（疲惫、恶心、疼痛、睡眠、食欲等），并在合适时提示您为复诊 **准备想问医生的话**。

**希望图书馆**  
来自多地区社区的希望与康复故事（可刷新获取新内容）；仅供鼓励与心理支持，**非医疗建议**。

**Quick SOS**  
一键进入 **5-4-3-2-1 grounding**，并可联系家人；也为您保留写下「暂时说不出口」的念头所需的空间。

**有何不同**

**隐私清晰**  
情绪与症状等记录默认保存在 **本机**。**心语**仅在您 **明确同意** 后将对话发往云端处理；不同意或离线时仍可使用 **内置话术**。**会话列表与历史保存在本机**，方便延续对话，而非宣称「全部 AI 都在手机本地」。

**情境感知**  
依时间与使用路径调整重点（如深夜更突出睡眠与温和陪伴）。

**触感与动效**  
配合原生触觉与流畅动效，让界面更稳、更可亲。
