XinBridge Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

XinBridge (“we”, “the app”) is designed for people affected by cancer. This policy explains what information we process, including when you use the AI chat feature.

1. Data you enter in the app

Mood check-ins, symptom logs, notes, and similar content are stored on your device unless you use a feature that explicitly sends data to our servers or to a third party (see below).

2. AI chat (OpenAI / cloud providers)

Heart Chat does not run AI on your phone. When you agree in the app, your messages are sent over the internet to a cloud AI service—typically OpenAI (ChatGPT API) outside mainland China, or a configured China-accessible OpenAI-compatible provider for Chinese language when set up. The following may be transmitted:

  • Your message text and a short recent conversation history (for context).
  • A system instruction so replies stay supportive, gentle, and aligned with XinBridge’s purpose.
  • Your app language (e.g. Chinese or English) so we can route to an appropriate provider.

We do not require you to create an account for basic use. Do not type passwords or full medical records into chat if you wish to limit what is sent.

3. Who receives AI chat data

  • Outside mainland China (typical): OpenAI, LLC (ChatGPT API), United States. Processing is subject to OpenAI’s terms and privacy policy.
  • Chinese language / configured China provider: When our backend is configured with a China-accessible provider (for example DeepSeek or another OpenAI-compatible API you deploy), that provider receives the same categories of data described above, under their terms.

Depending on your build, requests may go through our server or directly from your device to the configured provider (still a cloud AI service—the model does not run on your phone). Credentials for direct builds may be embedded in the app as set by your distributor. We do not sell your chat content.

4. Hope Library

Stories may come from public web search results, third-party search APIs (e.g. Serper), or curated demo content bundled in the app. We do not verify stories for accuracy, authenticity, or completeness. They are offered only in good faith to provide hope and emotional support. XinBridge has no obligation to ensure they are real, current, or medically accurate, and does not guarantee any outcome. This content is not medical advice. If you use the optional “refresh” feature, your request may be sent to our hosting provider and to those APIs as described in our deployment documentation.

5. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13 (or the age required in your region).

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer email you provide on the App Store or Play listing.

心桥隐私政策（摘要）

更新日期：2026 年 3 月

心桥为癌症相关人群设计。使用本应用即表示您已阅读本政策，尤其是 AI 心语 相关说明。

一、设备上的数据

情绪签到、症状记录等默认保存在您的设备本地。除非您使用明确需要联网的功能，我们不会自动上传这些内容。

二、AI 心语（OpenAI / 云端服务）

心语陪伴不在手机本地运行大模型。当您在应用内 同意 使用 AI 对话后，我们会通过互联网将信息发送至云端 AI 服务——通常为非中国大陆环境使用 OpenAI（ChatGPT API）；中文场景在已配置时使用境内可访问的兼容接口。可能传送的内容包括：

  • 您输入的文字及必要的近期对话上下文；
  • 用于保持温和、陪伴式回复的系统说明；
  • 应用界面语言，以便选择合适的服务区域与模型。

请勿在对话中输入银行卡密码、完整病历等极度敏感信息。

三、服务提供方

  • 一般情况（非中国大陆部署）： 可能由 OpenAI（ChatGPT API，美国）处理，并受其条款约束。
  • 中文场景且已配置国内可访问接口时： 可能由您配置的境内兼容接口（如 DeepSeek 等）处理。

根据构建与部署方式，请求可能经服务器转发，或由应用从设备直连至已配置的云端服务商（大模型仍在云端运行，不在本机）。若采用直连构建，分发方可能在应用包内配置接口凭证。我们不会将您的聊天内容出售给第三方。

四、希望图书馆

故事可能来自网络公开检索、第三方搜索 API、或应用内精选示例。我们不对内容的真实性、准确性或完整性进行核实或担保，仅以善意提供，用于心理支持与希望感。心桥无义务保证内容属实、及时或具有医学准确性，亦不构成医疗建议或疗效承诺。若您使用「刷新」等联网功能，请求可能经服务器转发至搜索类 API，详见部署说明。

五、联系我们

请通过应用商店页面所列支持邮箱与我们联系。
